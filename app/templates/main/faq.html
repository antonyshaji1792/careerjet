Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the CareerJet Autopilot and automation system.

Automation

How does the AI Autopilot work?

Our Autopilot uses a combination of scheduled scrapers and intelligent submission bots. It checks your "Job Alerts" periodically, matches findings against your profile, and automatically submits "Easy Apply" applications for jobs that meet your Match Score threshold. It even uses AI to answer complex form questions during the submission process.
Security

Is it safe to store my LinkedIn credentials?

Security is our top priority. Your password is never stored in plain text. We use AES-256 encryption via the Fernet protocol to encrypt your credentials before they ever hit our database. Only the background automation system has the temporary permission to decrypt them for the sole purpose of logging in to perform your requested searches.
Matching

How is the Match Score calculated?

The Matching Engine analyzes four key pillars:
  • Skills (40%): Word-level matching between your profile and the job description.
  • Roles (30%): Compatibility between your career goals and the job title.
  • Location (20%): Geographic alignment with your preferences.
  • Experience (10%): Check against years of experience requirements found in the text.
Automation

What is "Easy Apply"?

Easy Apply is a LinkedIn-specific feature that allows for streamlined applications without redirecting to a third-party ATS (Applicant Tracking System). CareerJet specifically targets these roles because they allow our AI to represent you accurately and complete the application in seconds.
Account Safety

Will LinkedIn ban my account for using this?

We have built-in safety features to mimic human behavior. Our bots use randomized "jitter" (waiting between actions), respect daily submission limits, and operate during standard human hours. However, we recommend setting a conservative daily limit (e.g., 5-10 applications) to ensure your account activity remains within normal patterns.
Profile

Can I use multiple resumes?

Currently, CareerJet uses your designated "Primary Resume" for all automated applications. You can upload multiple resumes in the Profile section, but you must select one as the primary to be used by the Autopilot.
Career Tools

How does the AI Video Interview work?

Our AI Video Interview uses advanced LLMs and emotion analysis to simulate a real interviewer. You'll face industry-specific questions, and the AI will analyze your verbal response, body language, and confidence, providing you with a detailed feedback report and improvement tips.
Career Tools

What makes the Resume Builder special?

Unlike traditional builders, ours is integrated with our Matching Engine. It scores your resume against live ATS (Applicant Tracking System) rules and suggests specific improvements to help you bypass automated filters at top-tier companies. It even helps you tailor your resume for specific job descriptions in one click.
