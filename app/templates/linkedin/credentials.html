❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔒 Is my password safe?

Yes! Your password is encrypted using AES-256 encryption (the same security banks use) before being stored. We never store your password in plain text, and we never share it with anyone.

🤖 What will CareerJet do with my LinkedIn account?

CareerJet will only use your credentials to: (1) Search for jobs matching your preferences, (2) Apply to jobs using LinkedIn's Easy Apply feature, and (3) Track your applications. We never post on your behalf or message anyone.

🔄 Can I change or remove my credentials later?

Absolutely! You can update your credentials anytime by coming back to this page. You can also delete them completely using the "Delete Credentials" button below.

⚠️ What if I use two-factor authentication (2FA)?

If you have 2FA enabled on LinkedIn, you may need to approve the login from your phone the first time CareerJet accesses your account. After that, LinkedIn will remember the device.

📧 What if I change my LinkedIn password?

Simply come back to this page and update your credentials with the new password. It takes just a few seconds!
